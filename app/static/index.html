About CropGuard AI

CropGuard AI is a free, open-source crop disease detection tool built to help Indian farmers protect their harvests. Simply upload a photo of a crop leaf and get instant AI-powered diagnosis.

  • Powered by MobileNetV2 deep learning model
  • Trained on 54,000+ PlantVillage images
  • Covers 38 disease classes across 14 crops
  • ~96% classification accuracy
  • Instant results — no sign-up needed
